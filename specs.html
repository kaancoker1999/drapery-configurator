Stack-back DRAFT

Pleat styleStack-back (per side, approx.)

Example: a 120″ split-draw Wave drapery stacks ~12″ per side; order wider than the glass if you want the glass fully cleared.

Draw configurations CONFIRMED

  • Split draw (center open) — two panels, opens from the middle.
  • One-way draw — single panel stacking left or right.
  • Stationary panels — decorative side panels, non-traversing.

Seaming & panel construction DRAFT

  • Maximum seamless panel width ≈ fabric width − side hems (typically ~52″ face on 54″ goods); wider panels are seamed at full fabric-width intervals.
  • Seams placed at fold valleys wherever possible; pattern-matched across seams on repeating fabrics.
  • Railroadable fabrics can run seamless up to the fabric width as height. PENDING — railroad list per fabric

Manufacturing tolerances DRAFT

  • Finished width: ±0.25″  ·  Finished length: ±0.5″
  • Hem alignment across a pair: within 0.25″

4 · Measuring, Installation & Construction

Customer-facing guides pending from manufacturer — interim guidance below

Measuring — quick guide DRAFT

  • Width: measure the area to cover; for full light control add 4–6″ each side past the frame (plus stack-back if the glass must clear).
  • Length (wall mount): from the rod/track pocket top to the desired end point — sill, below-sill (+4″) or 0.5″ above the floor.
  • Length (ceiling mount): from the ceiling to the desired end point.
  • All sizes to the nearest ⅛″; the factory cuts to the ordered finished size.
